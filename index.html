Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the (e)-mailing list?

Our mailing list is a great place to receive updates, chat with other families, and get involved in the community! You can join it now by clicking the link at the top bar of our page, or click on this Join link here!

When is the next event?

We have unfortunately had to stop meeting as frequently due to COVID-19 for the past year. We hope to get events going again soon though, so join our mailing list for frequent updates!

Where can I find more resources?

Our email list is a great place to share helpful information, events, and recommendations on anything from doctors to schools! We also have a lending library full of books, videos, and more that are very helpful resources to new members. You can find all of our resources on our resources page!

I'm not sure if I want to join the group and just want some information. What can I do?
