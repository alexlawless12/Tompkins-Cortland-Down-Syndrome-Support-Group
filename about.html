About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower and connect those with Down Syndrome and their families, and provide a community of shared resources and advice in the Ithaca, New York area.

What We Do

The Down Syndrome Support Group of Tompkins-Cortland Area is dedicated to helping families with children with Down Syndrome connect with other families. We share stories and advice, and build community with one another through planned events. Some of these include meetups at the park with our children, holiday themed events, or mom night outs. If you are interested in connecting with someone from the group, join our mailing list to learn more.
